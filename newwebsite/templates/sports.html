Footy

I've been playing football since as long as I can remember. I was very fortunate as I lived in a compound with friends that all enjoyed spending our time after school playing football, or basketball or volleyball or cricket (which despite being a Pakistani, I enjoyed the least). Obviously, my favorite was football, and it's what we played the most. Living in a compound however, means sharing the facilities with all age groups, which at the time was annoying but looking back, it likely aided in my ability to play as I would be playing football with people many years older than me in a small court. Anyway fast forwarding a couple years, I would be a couple years into training at an academy, playing with the under 18 and under 14 age group (2 separate trainings on the same day) 2-3 times a week. I love football.

Playing

As one does when playing a sport, they go to competitions. I went to many, both internationally and nationally. I competed many years at a national football competition called SIKAIC, and a decent amount of times internationally at a competition called BSME and with my acaedmy. My favorite of them all however was when i got the unique opprotunity to compete in the U19 SIKAIC at merely 14 years old and we won. I started every game, managed to score 2 goals and have a great time. It was also the first time that our school won this age group (i think).

Coaching

This initially started as a way to maintian my social life in the midst of all my studying and training. My friend and I decided to ask if we could help coach the U14 Sikaic team. Ofcourse, when a single teacher is training over 80 kids, help is very much welomed. so we did our part and came every week to trainings, not only assiting the coach but also designiing trainings based of what the team needed. That year the u14 won, so we coached next year again. Although he now is no longer in the country i continued to coach for the third year with another friend but this time the U16 team, and it was as enjoyable. I am currently coaching a local academy's (that i used to be a player at) U16/14 team to help prepare them from their league.

Running

I first got into running during covid, football academy was closed, but i needdd sport in my life. so i started running every morning. not going to pretend like i was naturally amazing because i wasnt. i ran 3 laps of my compound at the time and that was about 900m total. thats it. now at this current moment my longest run constantly is about 15 K but hopefully that will change soon.
